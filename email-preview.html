Key topics

Bitcoin as monetary infrastructure: Saylor argues BTC is not an investment but the foundation of a global digital capital network, inevitable for any sovereign treasury.

Nation-state competition: Once one G20 country accumulates a strategic reserve, others face a prisoner's dilemma — forced adoption or permanent debasement disadvantage.

The Lightning Network and layer-2 ecosystem evolve from payment rails into programmable capital channels, enabling sub-cent micropayments at scale.

21 million cap as constitution: Fixed supply isn't a feature — it's the entire value proposition. Any "upgrade" proposal is political capture, not technical improvement.

Market & price signals

Saylor's $13M per BTC target is derived from total global wealth (~$900T) converging onto a 21M-coin supply over a 100-year horizon — ~4.3% CAGR from current levels.

Short-term catalyst: US Strategic Bitcoin Reserve bill reintroduction expected Q3 2025; passage would trigger immediate sovereign FOMO buying across allied nations.

MicroStrategy's convertible-note model continues to outperform equity benchmarks — BTC-per-share NAV up 340% since initial purchase; institutional replication accelerating.

Actionable insights

Stack & hold: Dollar-cost averaging into cold storage remains the highest Sharpe-ratio strategy for retail allocators over 4-year time horizons, per Saylor's model.

Watch for the US Treasury's Q3 comment period on a Strategic Reserve proposal — a positive signal would be the single highest-conviction buy trigger in this cycle.

Avoid leverage: Saylor explicitly warns that 3× leveraged BTC products expose holders to volatility wipeouts that preclude participation in the eventual parabolic move.

Consider corporate treasury exposure via MSTR as a proxy if direct custody is operationally difficult — though self-custody remains the only way to hold actual BTC.

from Bitcoin Fundamentals (Preston Pysh)

Full summary & transcript

Key topics

Post-halving supply shock: New issuance dropped to 3.125 BTC/block; miner sell-pressure has historically peaked 60–90 days post-halving before reversing.

HODL waves show 70% of supply unmoved for 1+ year — historically a precursor to price discovery phases as available float contracts sharply.

Market & price signals

MVRV Z-Score: Currently at 2.1 — well below the 7+ readings seen at prior cycle peaks. Suggests significant upside before overheated territory.

Exchange BTC reserves hit a 5-year low of 2.3M BTC; reduced liquid supply amplifies price sensitivity to demand shocks.

Actionable insights

Pysh recommends monitoring the Puell Multiple weekly — a reading above 4 historically marks the optimal start of a DCA pause / partial-take-profit window.

Time horizon matters: Investors with <12-month windows face asymmetric volatility risk; the thesis is strongest for 3–5 year hold periods aligned with halving cycles.
